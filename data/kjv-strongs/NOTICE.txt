Files downloaded 2026-05-12 from canonical raw URLs:
- MetaV_MainIndex.csv    — per-KJV-word index: BookID, Chapter, VerseNum,
                            VersePos, WordID, Word, Italic, ...
- MetaV_StrongsIndex.csv — word-to-Strong's join: (WordID, StrongsID)
                            Multiple rows per WordID when a KJV word carries
                            compound Strong's tags.
- MetaV_Verses.csv       — plain KJV verse text (sanity / display source)
- MetaV_Strongs.csv      — Strong's lexicon: ID, lemma, transliteration,
                            pronunciation, description, POS, language

Use in this project:
- Substrate for the universal `atu_method/kjv_alignment/` module that
  distributes KJV English per source-language ATU line, anchored by
  Strong's matching between TAGNT/TAHOT source tokens and MetaV KJV
  words within the same verse.
- "Italic"-flagged KJV words (translator additions) attach to the line
  containing the nearest non-italic neighbor.
- Outputs (per-corpus eng-gloss-kjv/) inherit CC-BY-SA 3.0 attribution
  via this NOTICE.
